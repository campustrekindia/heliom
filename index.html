While beneficial, cannabis carries a risk of dependence, especially with heavy or long-term use. About 9% of users may develop Cannabis Use Disorder. Monitoring usage frequency is crucial.

üß† Cognitive Impact & Psychosis ‚ñº

High THC strains can temporarily impair memory and attention. Individuals with a predisposition to psychotic disorders (like schizophrenia) should avoid cannabis as it may exacerbate symptoms.

‚öñÔ∏è Legal & Medical Consultation ‚ñº

Cannabis laws vary globally. Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting cannabis therapy, especially if you are taking other medications, to avoid interactions.
